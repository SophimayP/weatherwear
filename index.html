Welcome to WeatherWear!

The aim of this web app is to provide a humanised and social view of weather data through suitable clothing for the day.

This is acheived through three different functionalities:

  • Check out the recommended clothing layers with data provided by the MetService.
  • See what others in your area are wearing.
  • Help us out and enter your own outfit of the day.

First make sure you have allowed your location and you can see your city in the top left corner or select a city manually using the geolocation icon next to the help icon. When you are ready click any of the three respective icons in the navigation at the bottom of the page to begin.

Thanks to MetService for weather data and Google for geolocation.
